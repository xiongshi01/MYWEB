China Three Gorges University (CTGU)

China Three Gorges University (CTGU) is located in Yichang city, Hubei Province, China. Having been approved by the Ministry of Education of China, CTGU comprises the former Wuhan University of Hydraulic and Electric Engineering/Yichang and the former Hubei Sanxia College. The history of CTGU can be traced back to 1923. The undergraduate education started from 1978, the postgraduate education and international education started from 1996.

CTGU has the qualification to confer the doctor's degree, to accept international students under Chinese government scholarship from China Scholarship Council(CSC), and to accept international students into undergraduate English medium program of Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) and to accept Chinese students from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan and other countries in the world.

CTGU, adhering to the principle of promoting development through opening up, organizes wide range of international cooperation and communication. It is the member of United Nations Academic Impact (UNAI) and the Standing Secretariat of Global University Network(N.E.W.S). CTGU has established cooperative relationships with more than 170 universities from more than 60 countries, CTGU, with 1400 international students currently, is a major destination in Hubei Province for international students and provides the largest scale of English medium education activities.

Hubei Provincial Scholarship Programs

"Hubei Provincial Scholarship" is established by Hubei Provincial Government, with a goal of encouraging more international students to study or do scientific research in Hubei provincial higher education institutes. With the approval of Hubei provincial government and Hubei Provincial Department of Education, China Three Gorges University(CTGU) recruits "Hubei Provincial Scholarship International Student" from all over the world to study or do research in CTGU.

For more information, please visit: https://eng.ctgu.edu.cn/info/1053/1982.htm

CTGU Scholarship Programs

To encourage more international students to study in China Three Gorges University, President Scholarship and College Scholarship are offered to new students, which covers full tuition fee or partial tuition fee with similar criteria as Hubei Provincial Scholarship; other regular fees shall be paid by students according to the university fees structure. From the second year, CTGU and the relevant colleges will offer some scholarships to excellent students according to their comprehensive ranking among all students. How Can You Be A CTGUer?

Register and submit your application at China Three Gorges University Online Application System for International Students: http://1sx.ctgu.edu.cn

;

If you apply for the Chinese Government Scholarship or International Chinese Language Teachers Scholarship (ICLTS), you need to submit your application at relevant system accordingly. Application system for CSC scholarship: http://studyinchina.csc.edu.cn;

Agency number of CTGU:11075;

Application system for ICLTS scholarship: http://cis.chinese.cn/account/login;

The admissions office shall make a preliminary examination of the applicant's materials. Those documents of the applicants who have passed the preliminary examination shall be sent to relevant colleges for academic review.

The Admissions office will submit the documents of the applicants who have passed the college academic review to China Scholarship Council or Center for Language Education and Cooperation or Department of Education of Hubei Province for a comprehensive review;

The Admissions office will submit the materials of the applicants who have passed the comprehensive review for system checking and issuing the electronic version of Information Form for Foreign Students'Short term Visit(DQ Form)or Confirmation Form for Study in China (JW201/JW202 Form);

The Admissions Office will send the admission documents to applicants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose HYMX Overseas Study Consulting Company?

We specialize in helping international students navigate the complex process of studying in China, with particular expertise in China Three Gorges University programs. Our team consists of current CTGU students who understand both the academic requirements and the student experience.

What makes China Three Gorges University special?

CTGU is located in the beautiful city of Yichang near the Three Gorges Dam, offering unique programs in hydraulic engineering and other technical fields. The university has strong international partnerships and offers many programs in English.

How competitive are the scholarships?

Chinese Government Scholarships are competitive but attainable with proper preparation. Our consultants can help you prepare the strongest possible application to maximize your chances of receiving funding.

What is the cost of living in Yichang?

Yichang offers a very affordable cost of living compared to larger Chinese cities. Students typically need about 1500-2000 RMB per month for basic expenses including food, transportation, and personal items.

Do I need to know Chinese to study at CTGU?

Many programs are offered in English, though basic Chinese skills will enhance your experience. For Chinese-taught programs, HSK level 4 is typically required. We can help you find appropriate language preparation resources.
